Chai Waale offers you a wide range of exotic-flavoured chai, in the most
hygienic ambience at pocket-friendly prices. The tea leaves are sourced
from some of the finest plantations in Assam. Our menu is not limited to Hot
and Iced Chai but also offers a lot to choose from our classic range
including filter coffee, cold coffee, and hot chocolate. We also serve quick
bites that go well with Chai along with a range of delicious freshly made
sandwiches, Maggi, momos, and a lot more.

Chai Waale is a fast-growing QSR chain headquartered in Chennai, India since 2018 with a fourth-generation promoter at its helm.

We are on a massive global expansion drive with new outlets opening soon in Dubai and the prestigious Presidency Club in Chennai, India.
